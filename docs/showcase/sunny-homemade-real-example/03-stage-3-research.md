# Stage 3: Research (Audience Insights & Market Data)

## What This Stage Produces

Research provides the foundation for strategy. It answers:
- **Who is our audience really?** (demographics, emotional drivers, pain points)
- **What are they searching for?** (keywords, problems they're solving)
- **What's working in the market?** (competitor moves, trend signals)
- **What did we learn last week?** (performance data, what resonated, what fell flat)

**Output format:**
- 1–2 pages of findings: audience data, performance trends, competitor notes
- Organized by: Demographics, Psychographics, Performance signals, Competitive context
- Actionable insights (not just data dumps)

### Performance Trends (From April–May Tracker Data)

**Format Performance:**

| Format | Views | Engagement | 3-Sec Hook | Conclusion |
|--------|-------|------------|-----------|------------|
| **Reels** | 1.9K | 31 interactions | High (50.8% thru-play) | Primary distribution engine |
| **Carousel** | Not tested this period | — | — | Risky, needs testing |
| **Static Posts** | 3.0K views | 83 interactions (low) | 490 3-sec views | Scroll-through, low engagement |

**Key insights:**
- ✅ Reels grew 255% week-over-week (strongest format)
- ✅ Static posts get reach but not engagement (people scroll, don't interact)
- ✅ Video watch time is high: 1h 55m total watch time on reels
- ❌ Static posts get 0 one-minute views (they don't hold attention)

**Product-Specific Performance:**

| Product | Best Angle | Views | Performance |
|---------|-----------|-------|------------|
| **Ginger Paste** | 3-dish utility showcase + nostalgia | 674 views (May 17) | Reach engine, feature 2x weekly |
| **Collagen** | Aging parent care (35 yrs after) | 397 views | Strong, but avoid repetition |
| **Chilli Paste** | (Was: generic spice focus) | 64 views | Underperforming, needs angle reset |
| **Curry Paste** | (New, soft-launch teaser) | TBD | Build anticipation, test interest |

**What angles work:**
- ✅ **Emotional hooks** (filial duty, heritage, family care) → High engagement
- ✅ **Reveal/discovery** (before-after, "what is this?") → High saves
- ✅ **Utility** (3-dish cheat sheet, time-saving) → High views
- ❌ **Generic product features** (ingredients, taste, texture) → Low engagement

### Last Week's Learnings (W05 Recap)

**What Worked:**
- ✅ Ginger paste = reach engine (highest views across all products)
- ✅ Reels = clear winner vs. static posts (255% growth)
- ✅ Emotional angles > product-feature angles
- ✅ 3-second hook is critical (50.8% rate = pattern interruption works)

**What Needs Improvement:**
- ❌ Chilli paste is underperforming (64 views) — needs angle reset (not "taste/heat" but "ease/convenience")
- ❌ Repetition kills: Posting collagen twice on same theme (systemic regeneration) caused fatigue
- ❌ Low messaging conversion (41 clicks → 10 messages) — CTA clarity issue
- ❌ Geographic concentration risk — 89% of reach from 2 states only

**Next Actions:**
- Reposition chilli paste (from "heat lover's choice" to "ready-to-eat convenience")
- Vary collagen angles (gut/sleep + aging parent care, not same theme 2x)
- Test new geographies after improving CTA clarity (Johor, Penang as secondary markets)

## Key Components of Research Stage

### 1. Audience Segmentation
- Not just "women 45-64"
- But: "Women 45-64 who value heritage, family care, time-saving solutions"
- Persona: Busy mother/grandmother with aging parents, wants to preserve family recipes, limited time

### 2. Performance Data Analysis
- Not just "reels perform better"
- But: "Reels grew 255%, static posts got 3.0K views but only 83 interactions (low engagement), video thru-play hits 50.8% with emotional hooks"
- Actionable: Commit to reels, analyze why static posts fail, replicate emotional hook formula

### 3. Competitor & Market Context
- What's the landscape?
- Is this a crowded market or opportunity?
- What pricing/positioning approach works?

### 4. Recent Performance Signals
- What did we learn LAST WEEK that changes THIS WEEK?
- What product/angle is trending up (double down)?
- What's trending down (reset)?
