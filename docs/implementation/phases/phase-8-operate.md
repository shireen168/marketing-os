## Skill 64: `/health`

**Project health audit. Monthly check-in.**
- Metrics: DAU growth (on pace?), retention (% of day-1 users on day-30?)
- Metrics: Revenue (on pace to sustain?), community (healthy, engaged?)
- Frequency: Monthly (Day 30, 60, 90, etc.)
- Action: Address problems before they become crises
- Output: Health report + action items

**Next:** Skill 65 (`/landing-report`)

---

## Skill 65: `/landing-report`

**Post-launch metrics dashboard. Retrospective at milestones.**
- Report: Downloads (actual vs forecasted)
- Report: CAC by channel (paid vs organic)
- Report: Retention curves (how long do users stick?)
- Report: Revenue (ARPU, churn rate, LTV)
- Insights: Which channel worked best? Which positioning resonated?
- Output: Metrics dashboard + learnings summary

**Next:** Skill 66 (`/document-release`)

## Skill 66: `/document-release`

**Changelog + release notes for every version.**
- Format: What changed? Why? What's next?
- Example: "v1.1: NEW offline tracking, IMPROVED battery (+20%), FIXED sync bugs"
- Distribution: App store, email to users, blog post
- Purpose: Build trust (users see continuous improvement)
- Output: Release notes + change log

**Next:** Skill 67 (`/retro`)

## Skill 67: `/retro`

**Retrospective + learnings capture. Quarterly.**

- Questions: What should be company doctrine moving forward?
- Team alignment: Everyone learns from everyone
- Output: Documented learnings + updated processes

## Skill 70 & 71: `/freeze` and `/unfreeze`

**Branch locking. Prevent accidental merges during major work.**

**Freeze:**
- Use: Before rewriting sleep algorithm (8-hour window)
- Effect: No merges allowed until `/unfreeze`
- Purpose: Protect stability during risky work

**Unfreeze:**
- Use: After major work complete
- Effect: Merges resume
