# In-House vs Outsource: Stage-by-Stage Breakdown

## What This Section Demonstrates

For each stage of the 6-stage framework, shows the decision: "Do this in-house or outsource to specialists?"

Sunny Homemade provides the real example of what was built in-house at SMB scale. The breakdown shows which decisions scale to enterprise (where more functions get outsourced).

---

## Quick Decision Framework

```
┌─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────┐
│ SHOULD WE BUILD THIS IN-HOUSE OR OUTSOURCE?             │
├─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────┤
│                                                           │
│ IN-HOUSE IF:                                             │
│ • Expertise is core IP (strategy, voice, positioning)   │
│ • Data flows into Tier 1 (research, performance loop)   │
│ • Cost is lower than agency (DIY + Claude Code)         │
│ • Speed matters (don't wait for vendor)                 │
│                                                           │
│ OUTSOURCE IF:                                            │
│ • Specialized skill rarely needed (video production)    │
│ • Quality premium > internal cost (design, photography) │
│ • Volume scales beyond team capacity (100+ posts/year)  │
│ • Not core to system (let agency handle, feed us data)  │
│                                                           │
└─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────┘
```

## Stage-by-Stage Breakdown

### Stage 1: Brief (Strategic Direction)

**Sunny Homemade:** IN-HOUSE

| Decision | Why | Cost/Time |
|----------|-----|-----------|
| Who writes the brief? | Founder (strategic thinking is core) | 30 min/week |
| Who validates research? | Founder (understands audience intimately) | 15 min/week |
| Tools used? | Claude Code + tracker data + market research | RM0 (tools already used) |

**Reasoning:**
- ✅ Strategic thinking is the core IP (not delegable)
- ✅ Founder knows audience better than anyone
- ✅ Research data is already tracked (no extra cost)
- ✅ Brief is short (30 min/week is sustainable)

**At Enterprise Scale:**
- IN-HOUSE still, but with team
- Regional CMOs write regional briefs (Americas brief, APAC brief, EMEA brief)
- Each feeds Tier 1 system (Claude Code coordinates all regional inputs)
- Time: 2–4 hours/week across team (not more expensive, just distributed)

---

### Stage 2: Voice (Brand Tone & Messaging)

**Sunny Homemade:** IN-HOUSE

| Decision | Why | Cost/Time |
|----------|-----|-----------|
| Who establishes voice? | Founder (understands brand intimately) | Initial 2–4 hours, then maintenance |
| Who maintains consistency? | Founder + content team (reference guide) | 30 min/week review |
| Who writes the voice guide? | Claude Code (synthesize from existing copy) | 1 hour |

**Reasoning:**
- ✅ Brand voice is core IP (reflects founder's personality + audience)
- ✅ Voice guide can be written once, referenced always
- ✅ Maintenance is lightweight (spot-check consistency)
- ✅ Inconsistent voice = brand drift risk (can't outsource)

**At Enterprise Scale:**
- IN-HOUSE (brand team owns voice)
- Voice guide becomes 50+ pages (brand guardrails for 100+ content creators)
- Regional variations documented (Mandarin vs. English tone rules, regional cultural norms)
- Time: 20–40 hours initial build, 5–10 hours/month maintenance

---

### Stage 3: Research (Audience Insights & Market Data)

**Sunny Homemade:** IN-HOUSE (with tools)

| Decision | Why | Cost/Time |
|----------|-----|-----------|
| Who tracks performance? | Founder + spreadsheet (built-in to workflow) | 15 min/week |
| Who analyzes trends? | Claude Code (automated trending, anomaly detection) | 10 min/week |
| Who synthesizes insights? | Founder (reviews Claude's analysis, adds context) | 20 min/week |

**Reasoning:**
- ✅ Performance data is already collected (Sunny Homemade tracker)
- ✅ Analysis is lightweight with Claude Code (not manual spreadsheet work)
- ✅ Data ownership stays in-house (competitive advantage)
- ✅ Fast feedback loop (data → strategy → next week's brief)

**What's Outsourced (if needed):**
- ❌ Paid market research (expensive, not needed yet)
- ❌ Competitor deep-dives (can do manually for SMB, not needed weekly)

**At Enterprise Scale:**
- IN-HOUSE core (proprietary data is competitive advantage)
- OUTSOURCE specialized research (win/loss analysis, brand tracking studies)
- Tools: Tableau, Looker for dashboards; Brandwatch or Semrush for competitive intel
- Time: 40–80 hours/month (data team, analyst roles)

---

### Stage 4: Strategy (Channel & Format Decisions)

**Sunny Homemade:** IN-HOUSE

| Decision | Why | Cost/Time |
|----------|-----|-----------|
| Who decides format? | Founder (based on performance data) | 15 min/week |
| Who plans content calendar? | Founder (high-level strategy) + Claude Code (detailed planning) | 30 min/week |
| Who allocates budget? | Founder (small budget, strategic oversight) | 10 min/week |

**Reasoning:**
- ✅ Strategy decisions are core (founder knows business goals)
- ✅ Format/channel decisions are data-driven (not subjective)
- ✅ Lightweight for SMB (one decision-maker)
- ✅ Changes weekly (too fast for external consultation)

**At Enterprise Scale:**
- IN-HOUSE (strategy team, not founder alone)
- Roles: Strategy director (overall approach) + channel managers (platform-specific)
- Cross-team coordination: Strategy informs product, sales, customer support
- Time: 60–120 hours/month

---

### Stage 5: Content (Captions & Design Specs)

**Sunny Homemade:** HYBRID IN-HOUSE + OUTSOURCE

| Component | Decision | Why | Cost/Time |
|-----------|----------|-----|-----------|
| **Captions (Writing)** | IN-HOUSE | Voice authenticity, brand consistency | 1–2 hours/week |
| **Image Generation** | OUTSOURCE (Gemini) | Faster than shooting, AI-quality sufficient | RM0 (free tool) |
| **Video Editing** | IN-HOUSE | Founder learns CapCut, keeps control | 3–4 hours/week |
| **Design/Polish** | IN-HOUSE (Canva) | Template-based, founder adjusts logo/text | 30 min/week |

**Reasoning:**
- ✅ **Writing:** Brand voice is core, must be in-house
- ✅ **Images:** AI generation is cost-effective at SMB scale (vs. photoshoot RM500-2000)
- ✅ **Video:** Learning curve small, investment modest (CapCut skills)
- ✅ **Design:** Canva templates reduce need for designer (vs. freelancer RM100-300 per post)

**When to Outsource Writing:**
- ❌ If volume exceeds 2–3 posts/week (context switching cost)
- ❌ If language consistency drifts (hire copywriter to maintain voice)
- ❌ If founder's time is better spent on Tier 1 (strategy > execution)

**At Enterprise Scale:**
- **Writing:** Team of copywriters (5–10) for 20+ posts/week, led by copy director for consistency
- **Images:** Professional photog + AI hybrid (Gemini for speed, photographer for hero shots)
- **Video:** Video producer or agency (CapCut becomes too manual for 100+ reels/month)
- **Design:** Design system + Figma templates + junior designers (not founder in Canva)
- Time: 200–400 hours/month (entire content operations team)

---

### Stage 6: Reports (Performance Analysis & Optimization)

**Sunny Homemade:** IN-HOUSE + TOOLS

| Decision | Why | Cost/Time |
|----------|-----|-----------|
| Who tracks metrics? | Founder + spreadsheet (built-in to workflow) | 20 min/week |
| Who analyzes performance? | Claude Code (automated reporting, variance analysis) | 15 min/week |
| Who synthesizes insights? | Founder (reviews Claude's analysis, makes decisions) | 20 min/week |

**Reasoning:**
- ✅ Performance data is owned in-house (competitive advantage)
- ✅ Analysis is pattern-recognition (Claude Code excels)
- ✅ Decisions are founder's (don't outsource business judgment)
- ✅ Lightweight with automation (not manual pivot tables)

**At Enterprise Scale:**
- **Data collection:** Automated (Tableau, Google Analytics)
- **Analysis:** Data team (2–3 analysts)
- **Reporting:** BI dashboards (self-service, not manual reports)
- **Insights:** Data team synthesizes, strategy team decides
- Time: 60–100 hours/month (analytics team)

## Quick Cost Comparison: SMB vs Enterprise

### Sunny Homemade (SMB, 1 person, 6 posts/week)

| Stage | In-House | Outsource | Total Cost |
|-------|----------|-----------|-----------|
| **Stage 1** | 30 min/week | $0 | ~RM0 (founder time) |
| **Stage 2** | 30 min/week | $0 | ~RM0 (founder time) |
| **Stage 3** | 45 min/week | $0 | ~RM0 (tools already used) |
| **Stage 4** | 45 min/week | $0 | ~RM0 (founder time) |
| **Stage 5** | 5 hours/week | RM0 (AI images) | ~RM100/month (Canva, CapCut, etc.) |
| **Stage 6** | 55 min/week | $0 | ~RM0 (tools already used) |
| **TOTAL** | 8 hours/week | — | **~RM100/month** |

**Total time + tools = 8 hours/week + RM100/month = ~RM2K/month equivalent (1 founder)**

### Enterprise (100M+ revenue, 20 posts/week, multi-regional)

| Stage | In-House | Outsource | Total Cost |
|-------|----------|-----------|-----------|
| **Stage 1** | 10 hours/month | $0 | ~RM20K/month (strategy team salary) |
| **Stage 2** | 15 hours/month | RM5K/month (brand agency retainer) | RM35K/month |
| **Stage 3** | 50 hours/month | RM10K/month (research tools) | RM50K/month |
| **Stage 4** | 60 hours/month | $0 | RM30K/month (strategy team salary) |
| **Stage 5** | 100 hours/month | RM50K/month (content agency, freelance writers) | RM150K/month |
| **Stage 6** | 60 hours/month | RM10K/month (BI tools, analytics) | RM40K/month |
| **TOTAL** | 295 hours/month | RM75K/month | **~RM325K/month** |

**Cost scales horizontally (more people) but architecture stays the same.**

## Key Insight: What Changes at Scale

**Stage 1-2-4-6: Core Strategy & Governance (Always In-House)**
- You can't outsource thinking (strategic direction, voice, decisions, analysis)
- At scale: Add team, but keep structure in-house

**Stage 3: Research (In-House Data + Outsource Specialist Research)**
- Performance data is in-house (competitive advantage)
- Specialized research (brand tracking, win/loss) can be outsourced

**Stage 5: Content (Hybrid, Varies by Component)**
- Writing: Always in-house (voice is core)
- Images/Video: Can outsource at scale (after processes are proven)
- Design: Outsource early (cheaper than hiring designer)

**The Pattern:**
- **Tier 1 (Strategy):** Always in-house (core IP)
- **Tier 2 (Outputs):** Mix in-house and outsource based on scale
- **Tier 3 (Execution):** Outsource aggressively (less core, more volume)

## Sunny Homemade's Hybrid Model

**What's truly in-house:**
- Stage 1 (strategic thinking)
- Stage 2 (voice, brand consistency)
- Stage 4 (strategy decisions)
- Stage 6 (performance analysis)

**What's outsourced to tools/AI:**
- Stage 5 image generation (Gemini = outsource, free)

**What's founder-executed but could be outsourced:**
- Stage 5 video editing (CapCut = doable solo, but hirable)
- Stage 5 design polish (Canva = doable solo, but hirable)

**If Sunny Homemade scaled to 3 posts/day (20+/week):**
- Hire: Video editor, content writer
- Keep in-house: Strategic direction, voice, data analysis, approval gates
- Result: Founder focuses on Tier 1 (thinking), delegates Tier 3 (execution)

## For the Showcase

### SMB Founders See

"I can start this alone (8 hours/week, RM100/month). As I grow, I hire people to do Tier 3 (execution), but I stay on Tier 1 (strategy). I don't need to hire a consultant permanently — I do it once, then I own it."

### Enterprise VPs See

"This consultant understands which work is core (keep in-house, always) and which can scale through hiring or outsourcing (execution). The system doesn't collapse if a team member leaves because core thinking is documented and processes are clear. We can hire junior people for Tier 3 without degrading quality."

---

## The Take-Away

**You don't have to do everything yourself at scale. But you have to own the thinking, the voice, and the approval gates.**

That's what makes the system scale without losing quality or control.
