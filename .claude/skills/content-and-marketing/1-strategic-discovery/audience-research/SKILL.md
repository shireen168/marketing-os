# audience-research

Deep dive into target audience behavior, preferences, pain points, and content consumption patterns.

## When to Use

Use this skill when you need to:
- Understand what your audience watches and engages with
- Analyze audience sentiment and discussion topics
- Extract insights from video content in your niche
- Identify content themes that resonate with your target market

## How It Works

This skill delegates to specialized tools:

- **Video transcript extraction:** Use `/youtube-transcript` to extract and analyze transcripts from YouTube videos in your niche
- **Sentiment and engagement analysis:** Analyze extracted transcripts for audience pain points, preferences, and engagement drivers

## Example Workflow

1. Identify 5-10 key YouTube videos your audience watches
2. Use `/youtube-transcript` to extract transcripts
3. Analyze for common themes, pain points, and language patterns
4. Compile audience profile with key insights
