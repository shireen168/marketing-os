# Skill: competitive-ads-extractor

## Trigger

Use when asked to "analyze competitor content", "what are competitors posting", "find winning content angles", or "competitive research for Sunny Homemade".

## How to Invoke

- "Analyze competitor content for Sunny Homemade"
- "What are competitors posting about [topic]?"
- "Find winning content angles in [niche/market]"
- "Competitive research for Sunny Homemade"

## Step 0: Load Reference Files

Before starting any Sunny Homemade competitive research, read:
- `references/sunny-homemade/brand-identity.md` -- audience (Chinese community, 30s-50s, Malaysia), brand voice, no Halal cert
- `references/sunny-homemade/content-rules.md` -- what Sunny Homemade will and won't do (content pillars, anti-hallucination, sign-off rules)
- Relevant product file(s) -- to understand the current messaging strategy before identifying gaps

This ensures recommendations don't conflict with Shireen's existing strategy.

## Context

Shireen runs Sunny Homemade, a Malaysian homemade food brand on Facebook and Instagram. Three products: Deep-Sea Fish Scales Collagen (RM10), Bentong Ginger Paste (RM11.90), All-purpose Chilli Paste (RM10.90). Target: Chinese community, 30s-50s, busy working adults. The market is saturated. The goal is to find what's working and identify angles competitors can't credibly own.

## Process

### Step 1: Define the competitive landscape
Ask (if not specified):
- What product/category are we analyzing? (e.g., homemade cookies, kuih, snacks)
- Local Malaysia market or broader?
- Facebook, Instagram, or both?

### Step 2: Research competitor content
Search for:
- Top-performing posts in the niche (high engagement indicators)
- What angles/hooks competitors are leading with
- What formats are dominating (reels, carousels, static posts)
- Common caption structures and CTAs
- Hashtags in use

### Step 3: Identify patterns and gaps

Analyze findings across three lenses:

**What everyone is doing (avoid or do better):**
- Generic angles that are oversaturated
- Cliched hooks ("Homemade with love", "Order now!")

**What's working but can be done differently:**
- Angles with strong engagement that Shireen can put a unique spin on

**What nobody is doing (opportunity):**
- Underexplored angles, formats, or perspectives
- Authentic or niche angles that bigger players can't credibly own
