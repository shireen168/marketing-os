# market-research

Analyze target markets, competitors, industry trends, and market size to inform product strategy.

## When to Use

Use this skill when you need to:
- Research market size, growth rates, and TAM/SAM/SOM
- Identify competitor offerings and positioning
- Analyze industry trends and market gaps
- Understand customer pain points and buying behavior

## How It Works

This skill delegates to specialized tools:

- **Deep market research:** Use `/deep-research` to search for current market data, industry reports, and competitive intelligence
- **Content extraction:** Use `/article-extractor` to pull structured data from articles, reports, and web content

## Example Workflow

1. Define your market and research questions
2. Use `/deep-research` with specific queries about market size, competitors, or trends
3. Use `/article-extractor` to pull key data points from discovered articles
4. Compile findings into a strategic brief
