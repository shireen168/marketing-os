# Marketing-OS Quickstart: Get Running in 10 Minutes

Welcome. You've got a complete marketing framework ready to deploy. This guide will have you running in 10 minutes.

## What is Marketing-OS?

A 4-layer system that teaches teams to run agency-quality marketing operations without agency retainers.

**Real results:** Sunny Homemade reduced weekly time from 5 hours to 2.5 hours while improving reach from 4.9K to 10,12K impressions and engagement from 0.6% to 4%+.

## The SCEO Framework (30 seconds each)

```
S: STRATEGIC DISCOVERY
   Market Intelligence, Competitive Analysis, Brand Positioning
         |
         v (Informs)
C: CREATIVE PLANNING
   Content Strategy, Campaign Planning, Copywriting
         |
         v (Produces)
E: EXECUTION
   Video Gen, Editing, Email/SMS, Social Scheduling, Analytics
         |
         v (Measures)
O: OPTIMIZATION
   Testing, Conversion Optimization, Strategy Iteration
```

Each layer builds on the previous one. You can't optimize what you haven't executed. You can't execute without a plan. You can't plan without understanding your market.

## Which Path Are You?

### Path 1: Marketing Manager (Building Team Strategy)
You manage content calendars, brand voice, and team output.

**Start here:** Layer 1 (Strategic Discovery) to define brand positioning and audience, then Layer 2 to plan content calendars.

**Key skills:** Campaign Plan, Content Calendar Strategy, Analytics Dashboard

---

### Path 2: Solopreneur (Running Solo)
You're doing your own social, email, content production.

**Start here:** Layer 3 (Execution) to automate video generation, scheduling, and analytics.

**Key skills:** Video Gen, Social Scheduling, Email/SMS, Analytics

---

### Path 3: Developer (Building AI Workflows)
You're integrating these skills into custom workflows.

**Start here:** [Skills Reference](../SCEO-SKILL-REFERENCE.md) to access the full skill library.

**Key skills:** All 22 skills, Router orchestrator, API documentation
