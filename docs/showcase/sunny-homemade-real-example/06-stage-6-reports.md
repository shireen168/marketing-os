# Stage 6: Reports (Performance Data & Optimization)

## What This Stage Produces

Reports close the loop. They answer:
- **What happened?** (actual performance data vs. targets)
- **What worked?** (which products, angles, formats, audiences)
- **What didn't?** (underperformers, learnings for next cycle)
- **What changes next?** (optimization recommendations for Tier 1 → Stage 1 of next cycle)

**Output format:**
- 1–2 pages: Performance summary, product breakdowns, learnings, next-cycle recommendations
- Data-backed (not opinions)
- Actionable (clear what to do differently)

**What Worked:**

- ⚠️ Recommend A/B test: Message objective vs. Website Visits objective (determine primary funnel)

**Next Action:** Analyze Linktree traffic (if available) to see if 41 link clicks → 30+ landed on Linktree, then only 10 proceeded to message (funnel drop-off on Linktree page).

## Variance Analysis

**Why was reach -12% below target?**
- Planned: 2.5K
- Actual: 2.2K (including 1.5K paid)
- Organic reach: ~700 views (vs. planned 1,000)

**Likely causes:**
- Algorithm changes (Meta reduced organic reach in this period for food content)
- No carousel testing (stick with reels only = less format variety reach)
- Geographic concentration (small market = ceiling on reach)

**Is this a problem?**
- No. Organic below plan, but paid exceeded plan (RM1.99 CPA vs. RM2.50 target)
- Net result: Campaign on target despite organic miss

**Next week:** If organic doesn't improve, consider carousel testing (20% carousel, 80% reels) to diversify reach.
