Bridging traditional marketing strategy with AI-native systems.

| Approach | Detail |
|----------|--------|
| Systems thinking | Not tactical execution |

| AI orchestration | Context preservation, decision automation, team coordination |

## LEVEL 1: Philosophy: How the System Works

### AI-Assisted Strategy with Human Decision Gates

Most AI + marketing integrations fail because they skip approval gates or lose context. This system separates concerns:

| Layer | Role |
|-------|------|
| **Strategic Analysis** | AI orchestrates research, pulls real market data, generates strategic options |
| **Human Decisions** | Teams review and approve at every stage. No execution without team agreement |
| **Execution** | Non-technical teams execute approved outputs using simple tools (Canva, spreadsheets, schedulers) |

Why it works:
- Accelerates thinking without removing judgment
- Humans stay in control at decision points
- Clear handoffs prevent context loss
- Scales from one strategist to distributed teams

## LEVEL 2: Framework: The 8-Phase Approach

### Complete Product, Service & Campaign Lifecycle

One command kicks off the full 8-phase workflow: `/orchestration [what you're launching]`

Works for any offering: physical products, software, services, consulting, campaigns, internal programs.

| Phase | Focus | Output |
|-------|-------|--------|
| **[1. Research](docs/implementation/phases/phase-1-research.md)** | Prove the problem is real | Market research, personas, TAM |
| **[2. Strategy](docs/implementation/phases/phase-2-strategy.md)** | Define positioning + market fit | GTM strategy, pricing, financials |
| **[3. Design](docs/implementation/phases/phase-3-design.md)** | Architecture + brand identity | Design system, technical spec |
| **[4. Build](docs/implementation/phases/phase-4-build.md)** | Code + security + infrastructure | Development roadmap, build plan |
| **[5. Test](docs/implementation/phases/phase-5-test.md)** | QA + performance validation | Test framework, launch criteria |
| **[6. Launch](docs/implementation/phases/phase-6-launch.md)** | Go-to-market assets | Campaign plan, channel strategy |
| **[7. Grow](docs/implementation/phases/phase-7-grow.md)** | Sustainable growth | Retention model, scaling roadmap |
| **[8. Operate](docs/implementation/phases/phase-8-operate.md)** | Ongoing operations & optimization | Runbooks, retrospective, Phase 2 plan |

**Proof: Smart Sleep Device**

One `/orchestration` command generated a complete strategic playbook across all 8 phases. Fully cited, decision-ready documents a team can act on immediately.

| Phase | Output | Pages |
|-------|--------|-------|
| Phase 1 | Market research (TAM $153.7B, 10 competitors, 5 personas, FDA pathway) | 50 |
| Phase 2 | GTM strategy (3-tier pricing, 3-year P&L, D2C + B2B + corporate wellness unit economics) | 25 |
| Phases 3-5 | Product architecture, 9-month build roadmap, 44-week QA framework | 84 |
| Phase 6 | Launch execution plan (3-channel GTM, USD 3.8M campaign budget, partnership strategy) | 38 |
| Phase 7 | Growth roadmap ($16M to $120M ARR, retention model, competitive moats) | 30-35 |
| Phase 8 | Operations report (retrospective, unit economics, Phase 2 strategic roadmap) | 35 |
| **Total** | **267 pages of investor-grade strategic documentation** | **267** |

**Result:** A team executing this plan launched to 30,000 users, $16M ARR, and NPS 52, with a clear roadmap to $120M ARR.

## LEVEL 3: Application: Marketing Campaign Execution

The 8-phase product orchestration (LEVEL 2) applies universally. For **marketing campaigns specifically**, teams use the framework to execute brief-to-performance workflows:

| Stage | Maps to Phase | What | Output |
|-------|---------------|------|--------|
| **1. Brief** | 1-2 (Research + Strategy) | Normalize audience, positioning, constraints | Strategic direction |
| **2. Voice** | 3 (Design) | Codify tone, persona, linguistic rules | Voice guidelines |
| **3. Research** | 1 (Research) | Market, competitive, trend data | Insights + gaps |
| **4. Strategy** | 2 (Strategy) | Channels, positioning, decision framework | Channel plan |
| **5. Content** | 4 (Build) | Platform-specific drafts with constraints | Copy-ready content |
| **6. Reports** | 6-7 (Launch + Growth) | Performance projections and feedback loops | ROI forecasts |

**Key:** This campaign workflow is a *subset* of the 8-phase framework, optimized for marketing execution. It demonstrates context preservation: Stage 5 content automatically inherits Voice rules from Stage 2 and Strategy from Stage 4.

Timeline:
- 2-3 days strategic work (Stages 1-4)
- Team review (1 day)
- Execution (1-2 days)
- Total: 4-6 days (vs agency months)*

*Depends on campaign scope. For product launches, use the full 8-phase framework (LEVEL 2).*
