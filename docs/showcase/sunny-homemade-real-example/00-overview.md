# Sunny Homemade: Real 6-Stage Example

## What Is It?

**The product:** Homemade Asian condiments and wellness products
- Ginger paste (Bentong origin, 100% ingredient)
- Sambal chilli (traditional recipe, no additives)
- Collagen (deep-sea fish scales, no sugar)
- Bak Zang (preserved vegetable, family recipe)

**The audience:** Primarily women 45-64, Selangor + KL
- Target emotion: Family care, heritage preservation, wellness
- Primary pain point: "I want to cook like my mom but don't have the time"

**The channels:** Instagram + Facebook (Meta ecosystem)

---

## Real Performance Data (April–May 2026)

### Overall Reach & Engagement

**Reels (All Reels Combined, Apr 5 – May 2):**
- Total Views: **1.9K** (↑ 255.6% week-over-week)
- Watch Time: 1h 55m (↑ 361.5%)
- Interactions: 31 (↑ 138.5%)
- Average Video Plays: 1.2K per reel
- Audience: 75% female, 45-64 age group

**Posts (All Posts Combined, Apr 5 – May 2):**
- Total Views: 3.0K (↑ 127.1%)
- 3-Second Views: 490 (↑ 375.7%)
- Content Interactions: 83 (↑ 25.8%)
- Total Viewers: 1,450 (↑ 182.7%)

**Insights:**
- ✅ Reels dramatically outperform static posts (255% growth)
- ✅ 3-second hook rate is strong (375% improvement)
- ✅ Audience is highly engaged (45-64F responding to emotional/heritage messaging)
- ⚠️ Static image posts get scroll-through without engagement (3.0K views, only 83 interactions)

### Paid Ad Campaign Performance (May 19–23, 2026)

**Campaign:** Bak Zang "Filial Duty" Angle
**Duration:** 6 days (RM5/day budget, total RM19.92)
**Audience:** Selangor 63% + KL 45%, Women 45-64

| Metric | Result | Performance |
|--------|--------|-------------|
| Views | 1.5K | Good reach for cold audience |
| Video Plays | 1.2K | High engagement |
| 3-Sec Thru-Plays | 610 | **50.8% hook rate** — strong pattern interrupt |
| Link Clicks | 41 | 2.89% CTR (reasonable) |
| Messaging Conversations | 10 | 0.67% conversion |
| **Cost Per Conversation** | **RM1.99** | Efficient acquisition cost |

**What Worked:**
- ✅ **Emotional hook landed:** "现在真的很少人会自己做子了" (heritage/filial duty) caught 50.8% of viewers
- ✅ **Tight geographic targeting:** 89% of reach concentrated in Selangor + KL (not scattered)
- ✅ **Efficient CPA:** RM1.99 per conversation is competitive for cold food products
- ✅ **Video format validated:** Reels with nostalgia angle outperform all other content types

**What Needs Improvement:**
- ⚠️ Low messaging conversion: 41 link clicks → 10 messages (leaky funnel, CTA clarity)
- ⚠️ Geographic concentration: 89% from 2 states limits scale (Johor 3%, Penang 2%)
- ⚠️ Post-engagement low: Ad reaches 1.5K but generates only 5 reactions + 1 comment
